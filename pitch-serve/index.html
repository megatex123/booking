The Problem

Car Servicing is Broken

😤

No Transparency

Customers don't know what work was done, what parts were used, or if they were overcharged.

📞

Friction-Heavy Booking

Phone calls, waiting, no confirmation — most workshops still operate with pen and paper.

🔍

Discovery is Broken

Finding a trusted mechanic relies on word-of-mouth. No ratings, no reviews, no accountability.

📊

Workshops Left Behind

Small workshops have no digital tools to manage bookings, inventory, or customer relationships.

5.7 million registered vehicles in Malaysia with no dominant digital platform for servicing.
